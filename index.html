Bio

Vile Creature is a two piece blackened doom metal outfit hailing from sunny Hamilton, Ontario. They'd prefer you call them a 9 piece blackened doom metal outfit but their 6 cats and 1 dog seem to not count, which is pretty rude to be honest. They take themselves very seriously, as you can tell from their serious website.
